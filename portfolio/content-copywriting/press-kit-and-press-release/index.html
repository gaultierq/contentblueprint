Press Kit and Press Release

A press kit and a press release are key tools for brand communication and media outreach.

Press Kit Definition: "A comprehensive resource that provides journalists and stakeholders with essential company details, product highlights, and visuals" to ensure accurate media coverage.

Press Release Definition: "A timely announcement designed to generate buzz around a product launch, partnership, or company milestone, delivering key information in a clear, engaging format."

Strategic Value

Together, these tools help brands accomplish three objectives:

  • Control messaging narrative — Ensure your brand story is told accurately and consistently
  • Build credibility with media and audiences — Provide verified information and professional materials
  • Maximize exposure opportunities — Make it easy for journalists to cover your story
